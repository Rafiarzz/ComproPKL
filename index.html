ABOUT

PT. Munggaran Jaya Utama (MJU) is a supplier and integrator of information and communication systems. MJU provides a variety of products to meet the needs of clients to get the most appropriate solution. We provide One Stop Solution in the field of information and communication and integrate its equipment systems (Tower Base Station, Microwave, Backbone, CCTV, Access Control, Alarm, Fire Alarm, MATV, IPTV, MEAT/PAGA, Video Conference, Video Wall, Wall Display, Passenger Information Display - PID) from survey, design, product, implementation to testing & commissioning of all equipment. We always present the best expertise, knowledge and quality of service to clients.
